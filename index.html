Why TerraVision AI?

The only open-source framework combining all four pillars of modern precision agriculture.

🛰️
Real-Time Earth Observation
Live Sentinel-2 NDVI extraction via Google Earth Engine at 10 m resolution with a 500 m analysis buffer and <20 % cloud filter. No static datasets.
⚡
ST-Transformer Inference
Multi-head self-attention (4 heads, d_k=16) learns crop-specific spatio-temporal signatures for wheat, rice, maize, and soybean globally.
☁️
IPCC Carbon Modeling
Integrated Cag = ŷ x 0.47 Mg C/ha estimator aligned with IPCC 2006 Vol. 4 (AFOLU) guidelines for above-ground biomass carbon.
🌍
Production Deployment
Zero-install public Streamlit app. Any agronomist worldwide can enter GPS coordinates and get a full yield + carbon report in under 10 s.
🔬
Peer-Reviewed Research
Full architecture details, training setup, and regional benchmarks available on full architecture details, training setup, and regional benchmarks.
📥
Downloadable Reports
Every inference generates a structured intelligence report with NDVI, yield, carbon, precision insights, and model metadata.
